Product Vision & MVP Scope

This section defines the core deliverables for the Minimum Viable Product (MVP). To ensure rapid market entry and academic feasibility, the scope is strictly focused on Image-to-Image (I2I) Virtual Try-On. This avoids the complexity of 3D modeling while leveraging high-fidelity Generative AI (GANs/Diffusion) to solve the "fit" problem for online shoppers.

⚡ Critical AI Requirement

The AI engine must prioritize two factors above all else: 1. Pose Preservation: The user's stance must remain identical to the uploaded photo. 2. Texture Mapping: Fabric details (lace, denim patterns) must be rendered realistically, not just colored over.

FYP Defense Strategy

For your Final Year Project report and presentation, focus on these three pillars. Academic projects are judged on methodology and evaluation, not just the final UI.

🎯

Focus on Accuracy

Don't just say "it works." Evaluate how the AI handles complex edge cases like lace textures, plaid patterns, and weird lighting. Qualitative analysis of these failure/success cases is crucial for the report.

📊

The Dataset

Explicitly mention the use of the VITON-HD dataset for training or fine-tuning. This is the industry standard benchmark and adds immediate academic credibility to your work.

⏱️

Performance Metrics

Measure your inference time. Aim for under 10 seconds per generation. If it takes longer, document your optimization strategy (e.g., model quantization or better GPU selection).
